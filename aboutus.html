About Us

Our Mission

The mission of the Greenfield CoderDojo is to help kids learn coding in a safe and social environment.

Our History

The Greenfield CoderDojo was founded in 2018 by Samantha Beauchamp, Jill Snyder, Greg Lipps, and Myron Snelson. We held our first session in March of that year and have continued ever since. During the height of the pandemic, we held our sessions virtually.

The Foundation

We are a Dojo in good standing with the CoderDojo Foundation. The CoderDojo program was started over ten years ago in Ireland.
