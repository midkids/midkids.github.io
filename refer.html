How to refer a friend

We would like to reach as many Hancock County kids as possible. If you have a friend who is interested in coding, please refer them to our Dojo by:

  • Forwarding our email address to them: greenfield@coderdojo.com
  • or
  • Forwarding our website address to them: http://msnelson.s3-website-us-east-1.amazonaws.com/FinalProject/

Thank you!
