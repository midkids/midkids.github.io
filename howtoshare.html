How to share a Scratch project

In order to share a Scratch project, you must have created a Scratch account and followed the instructions to confirm your new account on the email sent to you by the Scratch system.

  1. Go to scratch.mit.edu in a browser
  2. Login into your Scratch account
  3. Find and open the project you would like to share in the My Stuff folder and click on the See inside button
  4. If you have not already shared your project, click on the Share button to share it
  5. Click on the Copy Link button
  6. Click on Copy embed in the window that opens
  7. Create an email to greenfield@coderdojo.com
  8. Make the subject of the email Showcase
  9. Paste the copied embed code in the body of the email
  10. If you would like to have your name by your project in our Showcase, please include your name (first name only!) under the pasted embed code
  11. Send the email

How to share an Arcade project

In order to share a Arcade project, you must have a created a Microsoft account and followed the instructions to confirm your new account on the email sent to you by Microsoft.

  1. Go to arcade.makecode.com in a browser
  2. Login into your Microsoft account
  3. Find and open the project you would like to share in the My Projects section and click on it
  4. Click on the share icon at the top next to the home icon
  5. Click on the Publish project button
  6. Click on > Embed in the window that opens
  7. Click on the Simulator tab in the embed section
  8. Click on the Copy button in the embed section
  9. Create an email to greenfield@coderdojo.com
  10. Make the subject of the email Showcase
  11. Paste the copied embed code in the body of the email
  12. If you would like to have your name by your project in our Showcase, please include your name (first name only!) under the pasted embed code
  13. Send the email
