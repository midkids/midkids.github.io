Who and What

Who should attend?

CoderDojo is open to students aged 7-17. We have kids who attend public school, kids who attend private school, as well as kids who are home schooled.

You do not have to have any coding experience! However, we do split into two groups. The first group is for kids with some or no coding experience. The second group is for kids with more coding experience.

What will I learn?

The first group of coders will learn coding through some introductory Scratch projects. Scratch is an engaging, block-based programming language that is perfect for learning coding without requiring much typing. But don't be fooled, Scratch is an extremely powerful programming language.

The second group of coders will learn more advanced coding using Microsoft MakeCode Arcade projects. Arcade includes advanced block-based coding, but also allows for JavaScript coding. JavaScript is text-based and is great for learning your first your professional language after mastering Scratch. You will need to know how to type to join this group.

What should I bring?

Please bring a fully charged laptop computer. The main seating area of the Ninestar Connect Conference Center does not have electrical outlets, but we will have charging tables on the sides of the room. If you do not have access to a laptop, we have 10 Chromebooks available for use, first come, first served. Please indicate on your registration if you would like to borrow one of our Chromebooks.
