Events::Event FAQs

What is CoderDojo?
CoderDojo is an international non-profit organization whose mission is to provide students worldwide the opportunity to learn coding and to be creative in a safe and social environment...for free!
Who should complete the registration?
A parent/caregiver can complete the registration for the coder (child), or a coder 13 years of age or older can complete it themselves. Up to four tickets can be reserved during registration.
Whose information goes where in the registration?
If a parent/caregiver completes the registration, they should enter their name and email address in the Contact Information section, and then enter their coder's name in the Ticket 1 - Coder section. The email address in the Ticket 1 - Coder section can be that of the parent/caregiver or the coder.

If a coder completes the registration, they should enter their parent/caregiver's name and email address in the Contact Information section, and then enter their name and email address in the Ticket 1 - Coder section.
Is the number of coder tickets limited?
Due to COVID-19, the number of coder tickets is limited to 25, but we will have a wait list in case any coder cancels.
What age must I be to participate in the Greenfield CoderDojo Live event?
Coders must be between the ages of 7-17.
Do I need a laptop computer to participate?
If you have access to a laptop computer, please bring it with the battery fully charged. We will have a few charging tables around the outside of the conference room. If you do not have access to a laptop computer, we will have 10 Chromebooks available for use, first come, first served.
How much does it cost to attend The Greenfield CoderDojo Live event?
It is completely free and even includes a snack!
Do I need to know coding to attend?
No, you do not have to have any coding experience to join us. However, you will go home a coder!
What is Scratch?
Scratch is a free programming language and online community where you can create your own interactive stories, games, and animations. If you have little or no coding experience, no problem! Come on in and learn an little bit about Scratch with us!
What is Microsoft MakeCode Arcade?
Microsoft MakeCode Arcade is a 2D pixelated game development environment that is free and entirely online. Students can design their own game characters and code their behaviors using Blocks or JavaScript. If you know Scratch and are ready to move on, join us for a next step in coding!
What time should I arrive?
You should plan on arriving a few minutes before 1 pm EST. It takes a few minutes for us to check you in, check out a Chromebook if you need one, and borrow a mouse if you want one.
Will you provide food?
Well, sort of. Snacks will be provided for all coders and their parents/caregivers who stay for the event.
Where should I park?
There is plenty of free parking at the Ninestar Connect Conference Center.
What doors should I enter?
Please enter the doors on the southwest side of the conference center by the patio.
What if I have other questions?
Please contact the Champion of the Greenfield CoderDojo at greenfield@coderdojo.com.
What if I register, but later determine I cannot attend?
Please email the Champion of the Greenfield CoderDojo at greenfield@coderdojo.com as soon as possible. The number of attendees is limited due to COVID-19. Giving up your ticket will possibly allow us to invite another coder on our wait list.
