Learn to code...for free!

Please joinThe Greenfield CoderDojo for live and in person events each month at the NineStar Connect Conference Center, sponsored by the NineStar Connect Nerds. If you are between the age of 7-17, come show or learn some coding skills, have fun, eat a snack, and maybe make a new coding friend or two...always for free!

  • Learn coding in a safe and social environment
  • We will prove to you that you can code
  • You will not just play games, you will create them
  • You will learn perseverance as you debug your programs
  • You will prepare yourself for the computer science courses offered in your schools

Our primary concern is for the whole child, not just coding. Our most important goal is for all our coders to leave feeling a little bit better about themselves.
