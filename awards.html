Events::Awards

Awards will be earned for attendance at our Dojo events. Earned awards are given at the next event attended. The events attended do not need to be consecutive. This awards program will end on September, 2022.

Eligibility Award
Start of first visit: Super Ned the Nerd sticker
Attend 1 event: code.org sticker
Attend 2 events: $5 Amazxon gift card
Attend 3 events: micro:bit case
Attend 4 events: code.org t-shirt or hat
Attend 5 events: micro:bit accessory kit
Attend 6 events: micro:bit Elecfreaks Mini Cutebot Kit
Attend 7 events: micro:bit version 2 Go Kit
Attend 8 events: Kittenbot Meowbit
