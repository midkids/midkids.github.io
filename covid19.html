Events::Covid-19 Safety Measures

  • Attendance at these sessions will be limited to 25 coders
  • The Ninestar Connect Conference Center will be cleaned before the event
  • Masks are optional
  • Tables for coders will be kept approximately six feet apart, but coders from the same family may sit at the same table or move their tables together if desired
  • All close (less than six feet) interactions between coders, parents/caregivers, and volunteers should be limited to five minutes or less
  • If a coder or any member of their family is experiencing any of the following symptoms, they should not attend the event: fever or chills (temperature greater than 99.9), sore throat, cough, shortness of breath, difficulty breathing (especially new onset, uncontrolled cough), diarrhea, nausea, vomiting, abdominal pain, headache (particularly new onset of severe headache, especially with fever), new loss of taste or smell

Currently, per the Indiana COVID-19 Dashboard, Hancock County is in yellow status. Should it change to red status at any time before this Greenfield CoderDojo Live event, the event will be canceled and all registered coders will be notified.
